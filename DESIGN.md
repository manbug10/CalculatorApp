## 📐 Dimensiones

- **Button Size**: 64dp × 64dp (Basic mode)
- **Button Spacing**: 4dp
- **Corner Radius**: 16dp (buttons), 20dp (panels)
- **Display Font**: 48sp (result), 20sp (expression)
- **Button Font**: 24sp

## ✨ Animaciones

1. **Button Press**: Scale down to 0.95, ripple effect
2. **History Panel**: Slide in/out vertically
3. **Result Update**: Fade transition
4. **Theme Change**: Cross-fade animation

## 📱 Responsive Design

- **Portrait**: Standard layout
- **Landscape**: Side-by-side (display | keyboard)
- **Tablet**: Larger buttons, expanded scientific panel
